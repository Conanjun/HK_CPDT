Credit Ranking
Credit Ranking shows the credits ranking of the learners. The learner can view the credit ranking through
Credit Ranking gadget of homepage or My Training > Learning Center > Credit Ranking menu.
Credit Ranking gadget on learner’s homepage:

As the above screen:
  1. Credit is calculated by the rule set by System Administrator or Training Instructor.
  2. It shows the only top 10 learners and their credits.
  3. Click to extend or retract the credit list.
  4. The learner can click More… to view more Credit Ranking.

Click My Training > Learning Center > Credit Ranking menu, the following screen will be shown:

As the above screen:
Total Credits: {X}/Your Current Ranking: {Y} - it shows the total number of credits in the Credit Ranking and the current ranking of the Learner.
Last Update - the last update time of the Ranking. Please note that the ranking is updated periodically.
