As the above screen:
  1. :click it to extend or retract the announcement list.
  2. :it means that the announcement is published in the nearest 3 days.

Each Training Center has its own announcement, the learner can only see the announcement of his/her Training Center. Each TAB on the top of the announcement list represents one Training Center, the learner can click the TAB to view the announcement of a particular Training Center.
