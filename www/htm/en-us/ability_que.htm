If the learner is required to participate one or more Appraisal Forms, he/she will see them on the top left corner of homepage, please refer to the following screen.

  1. It only shows the Appraisal Forms that specify the learner as Assessors.
  2. Each Appraisal Form can be submitted only once.
  3. Due Date - the last submission date of the Appraisal Form, please note that the Appraisal Form will not be shown after the Due Date.
  4. Click the Appraisal Form Title to start it, please refer to the following screen:

Click Save to save but not submit the Appraisal Form, click Submit to submit it, or click Cancel to return.
Note: The Appraisal Form will not be shown after it is submitted.
