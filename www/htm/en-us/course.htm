Training Catalog
Courses are published to the Training Catalog, the Learner can access to Training Catalog to view the courses that publish to him/her. He/she can enroll them if he/she is interested.
Click Browse Catalog under Training Catalog menu, the following screen will be shown:

You can change the Training Center tab, training catalog, or use the simple search / advanced search to view different courses.
Click the course title to view details of the course, please refer to following screen:


As the above screen:
  1. Enrollment period: the period that the learner can enroll in the course.
  2. Enroll: Click Enroll and follow the guide to enroll the course, after this, the enrollment status will shown at the bottom of this page.
  3. Add to Training Plan: Add the course to My Training Plan.

Note: Add to Training Plan button will NOT be shown if the course is already in My Training Plan module.
