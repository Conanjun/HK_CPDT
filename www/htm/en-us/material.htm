Knowledge Center
Learner may have accessibility right released by the System Administrator or Training Instructor to view the Resource Folders and their resources such as SCORM, AICC and NETG courseware.
There are two entrances to the Knowledge Center, Quick link on the homepage and Knowledge Center under Community menu.
On the bottom left corner of homepage, the Knowledge Center gadget will be shown:

As the above screen:
  1. It shows the resources folders under the learner’s Training Center that publish to learners.
  2. The titles in blue are the topmost level resource folders. The number in bracket shows the total number of resources under that folder. The titles in black under the topmost level resource folders are their sub-folders.
  3. The learner can view the resources of the resource folder by clicking the Folder Title, or click More… to the homepage of Knowledge Center.
  4. Click button to extend or retract the resource folders list.

Click Knowledge Center under Community menu from homepage, the following screen will be shown:

As the above screen:
  1. TABs on the top left corner are the Training Centers that the learner belongs to (please note that a learner can belongs to multiple Training Centers). It shows the knowledge of the learner’s nearest Training Center. Click the other Training Center TAB to view the knowledge of that Training Center.
  2. Click the resource folder title under By Catalogs to view the resources of that folder, click the resource title under the Knowledge List to view the content of that resource.
  3. The learner can search knowledge by Search, Advanced Search or Narrow the search functions provided in the Knowledge Center homepage.
