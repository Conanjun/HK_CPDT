Ranking
Click Ranking under Training Catalog menu, the following screen will be shown:

There are 3 types of Ranking: Hot, Best and New.
  1. Hot: it shows the most popular courses according to the number of enrollments. The list will order by the total number of enrollments in descending order (the most the first).
  2. Best: it shows the most appropriate courses for the learner according to the Rating. The list will order by Rating in descending order (the most the first).
  3. New: it shows the latest courses according to the creation time. The list will order by Creation Time in descending order (the latest the first).
