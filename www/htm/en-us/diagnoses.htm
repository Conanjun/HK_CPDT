Questionnaire
After the Training Administrator publishes a questionnaire to the learners, a Questionnaire gadget will be shown on the top left of the learners’s homepage, please refer to the following screen:

As the above screen:
  1. The learner will only see the questionnaires that publish to him/her and the current date is during the Publish Date.
  2. Questionnaire can be submitted only once.
  3. Due Date – the due date is only shown before the questionnaire is submitted.
  4. Click the questionnaire title to start, the following screen will be shown:


Answer the questions and click Submit to submit the questionnaire, or click Cancel to return.
Note: The questionnaire will not be shown anymore after it is submitted.
